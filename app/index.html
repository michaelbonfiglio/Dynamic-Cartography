Dynamic Cartography

An exploration of ways to inject life and motion into maps.

You are here.

This website is the home of Dynamic Cartography, an exploration of breathing life into representations of our world by combining animation, maps, and graphic design. It was created by Michael Bonfiglio at the Maryland Institute College of Art. Here, in the pages of this site, you will find: video animations, interactive web maps, and documentation of the process and experience of making this project come to life.

Dynamic Cartography is the thesis project of Michael Bonfiglio, created for the Graphic Design MFA at MICA.
