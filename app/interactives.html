Dynamic Cartography

An exploration of ways to inject life and motion into maps.

Interactives

The great majority of mapping activity lives in services and apps like google, yahoo, and apple maps. Because I’m intrigued by the interactivity and user-driven affordances that these technologies provide, I wanted the chance to try to add my graphic methods to the modern landscape of digital mapping services. The below was created with Leaflet’s API, Mapbox, and NOAA map data.
